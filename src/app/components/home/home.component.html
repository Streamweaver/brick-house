Teaching Blockchain Concepts

Brick House is a small application aimed at breaking down the elements that make up the block-chain ecosystem into small, understandable concepts. Each concept has a section that includes a small online exercise to reinforce those concepts. Linking these concepts together will provide an overview of the underlying functions that make blockchain technology like BitCoin or Ethereum work.
